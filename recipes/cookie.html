Chocolate Chip Cookies

Yield: 3 dozen (Source)

Things you'll need:

    Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 sticks salted butter (Room Temp)
  • 2 large eggs

    • Equipment:

  • Hand Mixer
  • Non-stick baking sheet

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375F (190.6C) degrees
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda and salt
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar and vanilla extract until light and fluffy
  4. Add the eggs one at a time mixing well in between each addition
  5. On low speed, slowly add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips
  7. Place 2 tbsp of dough onto the baking sheet 2 inches apart
  8. Bake in the oven for 7-9 minutes
  9. Let it sit on the tray for 1 minute to cool
  10. Transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely
