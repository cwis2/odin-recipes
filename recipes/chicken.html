Chicken Thighs

Yield: 8 servings (Source)

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce dried chipotle chile pepper
  • 1 ounce dried ancho chile pepper
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 red onion, cut into small chunks
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Procedure:

  1. Place chipotle and ancho chile peppers in a shallow bowl; pour in water
  2. Cover the bowl and let sit at room temperature until peppers are softened (10-12 hours)
  3. Drain water and remove seeds from peppers
  4. Blend chile peppers, red onion, garlic, sea salt, cumin, oregano, and black pepper in a blender until a coarse paste forms; add olive oil and blend until marinade is smooth.
  5. Place chicken thighs between 2 sheets of heavy plastic on a solid, level surface
  6. Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a 1/2-inch thickness
  7. Place chicken in a resealable plastic bag and add marinade
  8. Turn chicken several times to coat
  9. Seal bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours
  10. Preheat an indoor grill with top and bottom plates for medium-high heat
  11. Remove chicken from the bag and discard marinade
  12. Place chicken on the preheated grill, close the lid, and cook until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear (5-7 minutes)
  13. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165F (74C) degrees
  14. Cut chicken into strips and serve warm
